The World Between Worlds

Limbo is a haunting, minimalist puzzle-platformer that follows a nameless boy as he ventures through a dark, monochrome world in search of his missing sister.

With no dialogue or instructions, players must navigate eerie environments filled with deadly traps, hostile creatures, and physics-based puzzles, learning through trial, failure, and discovery.

Stark black-and-white visuals, ambient sound design, and open-ended storytelling create a deeply atmospheric experience where meaning is left to interpretation, making every step feel tense, mysterious, and unforgettable.
