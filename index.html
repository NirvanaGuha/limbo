The World Between Worlds

Limbo is a haunting, minimalist puzzle-platformer that follows a nameless boy as he ventures through a dark, monochrome world.

Stark black-and-white visuals and ambient sound design create an experience where every step feels tense and mysterious.

The Weight of Silence

In the silence, the shadows begin to move. The deeper you go, the more the world pushes back.

With no dialogue or instructions, players must navigate eerie environments filled with deadly traps and hostile creatures.

Gravity is your only friend, and your greatest enemy.
